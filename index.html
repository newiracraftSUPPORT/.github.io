Common Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through Apple App Store or Google Play. Open your device's subscription settings, find the app, and tap Cancel Subscription. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.
How do I delete my account and data?
Email us at support@newiracraft.com with the subject "Delete My Account" from your registered email. We'll complete the deletion within 30 days.
The app isn't working — what should I do?
Try closing and reopening the app. If issues persist, make sure you're on the latest version from the App Store or Play Store. Still not working? Email us with your device model and OS version.
Can I request a refund?
Refunds are handled by Apple or Google depending on where you purchased. Visit Apple's Report a Problem page or Google Play's refund support page to submit a request.
Is my data private?
Yes. We collect only what's needed to run the service and never sell your data. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.

Last updated: 1 June 2025

newiracraft ("we", "us", or "our") operates mobile and web applications including PostEasy and other products. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our apps and services.

By using our apps, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information: Name, email address, and password when you create an account.
  • User-generated content: Reviews, drafts, and text you enter into our apps.
  • Communications: Messages you send us via support or feedback forms.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data: Features used, session duration, in-app actions, and crash reports.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, unique identifiers, and app version.
  • Log data: IP address, access times, and screens viewed.

1.3 Information From Third Parties

  • Authentication providers: If you sign in via Google or Apple, we receive your name and email from those providers.
  • Payment processors: Subscription payments are handled by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not store your payment card details.

We use your information to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve our apps and services.
  • Process and fulfil your subscription.

Some of our apps use AI services (such as Google Gemini) to process text you submit — for example, to enhance review drafts. Text submitted for AI processing may be sent to third-party AI providers under their respective privacy policies. We do not use your content to train AI models without explicit consent. You should not submit sensitive personal information through AI-powered features.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers: Trusted third parties assisting us in operating our services (e.g. cloud hosting, analytics, AI APIs), bound by confidentiality agreements.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us below. We will action deletion requests within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

6. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. No internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 where required by local law). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.
